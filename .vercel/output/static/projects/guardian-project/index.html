Guardian Project

An award-winning, open-source mobile security collaborative developing tools for activists, journalists, and humanitarian workers worldwide.

Secure Technology for Human Rights

We develop, test, and distribute software that enables secure, private communications in the most challenging environments.

Keanu Engine

Offers app and web experiences that enable safe and secure communication among communities.

Butter Box Offline App Store

Broadcasts its own WiFi network to share apps and join public or private chatrooms without internet.

ProofMode

Capture, share, and preserve verifiable photos and videos with cryptographic proof.

Circulo

Connect to a reliable network of six peers. Establish protocols, send alerts and keep your circle informed.

LetsConvene

An easy-to-use, ephemeral, and privacy-preserving communications and information sharing tool.

Flagship Application

Orbot: Tor for Android

Orbot is a free proxy app that empowers other apps to use the internet more securely. Orbot uses Tor to encrypt your Internet traffic and then hides it by bouncing through a series of computers around the world.
