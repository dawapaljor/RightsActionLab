Our Mission

Advancing Rights Through Innovation

Rights Action Lab supports mission-aligned projects that advance human rights for people living under extreme repression by harnessing the power of secure digital communications tools with creative nonviolent activism and education.

Who We Are

Staff and collaborators include strategists, developers, campaigners, public intellectuals, researchers and facilitators. We facilitate linkages and support programs that provide education and training, research and advocacy, technology development and digital security outreach.

"We believe that the combination of innovative technology and strategic nonviolence is the key to addressing rights abuses in the most repressive conditions."

What We Do

  • Educational programs on Internet freedom, digital security and protecting against spyware

  • Evidence-based research to advance campaigns and support the development of freedom-enhancing technologies

  • In-depth training on strategic organizing and advocacy, and the application of nonviolent strategies

  • Development of new software that enables secure, private communications for rights defenders, journalists, and activists

  • Network building between communities facing extreme human rights violations
