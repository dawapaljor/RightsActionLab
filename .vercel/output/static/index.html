Rights Action Lab

Advancing Human Rights in Repressive Environments

We provide training, research, and technology to empower activists and communities facing extreme repression and human rights abuses.

Our Mission View Projects

Rights Action Lab is a nonprofit 501(c)(3) charitable organization dedicated to human rights and digital security.

We facilitate linkages and support programs that provide education and training, research and advocacy, technology development and digital security outreach.

Education And Outreach

Carry out educational programs on internet freedom, digital security, and strategies of nonviolent advocacy for Tibetans and other communities facing extreme repression.

Training

Provide in-depth training in digital security, civil society organizing, strategic advocacy, and application of nonviolent strategies to catalyze positive change.

Research

Evidence-based research in multiple languages using sound methodologies and secure practices. Our research drives high-impact campaigns.

Technology Development

Develop, test, distribute and promote new software that enables secure, private communications for activists, journalists, and rights defenders.
