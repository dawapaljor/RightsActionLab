Rights Action Lab is a nonprofit 501(c)(3) charitable organization dedicated to human rights and digital security.

We facilitate linkages and support programs that provide education and training, research and advocacy, technology development and digital security outreach.

Education And Outreach

Running educational programs on internet freedom, digital security, and strategies of nonviolent advocacy for Tibetans and other communities facing extreme repression.

Training

Providing in-depth training in digital security, civil society organizing, strategic advocacy, and techniques of nonviolent action.

Research

Conducting evidence-based research in multiple languages using sound methodologies and secure practices. Our research drives high-impact campaigns.

Technology Development

Developing and distributing software that enables secure, private communications for activists, journalists, and others seeking freedom of expression.

Building Networks

Building networks between and capacity of communities facing extreme human rights violations.
