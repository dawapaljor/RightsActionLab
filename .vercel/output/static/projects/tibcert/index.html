Current Project

TIBCERT

The Tibetan Computer Emergency Response Team (TibCERT) counters cyber attacks from powerful threat actors, serving as a model for impacted communities worldwide.

China Index

The first cross-regional project to objectively measure and visualize the People‘s Republic of China (PRC)‘s overseas influence through comparable data.

Tibet's Voice in Cyber Security

Positioning Tibet on the global cybersecurity stage, highlighting China's censorship and surveillance in Tibet and transnational digital repression.

TibCERT Response Hub

A volunteer-based community platform built on the structure of Learn, Share, Network and Support to counter cyber attacks.

Countering Transnational Repression

TibCERT effectively counters sophisticated cyber attacks, providing ground-breaking models for digital defense. Our work highlights the regime’s digital repression of the diaspora and strengthens community resilience.
